The Philosophy

Hourly Rates Serve the Tutor. Monthly Commitment Serves the Learner.

When you pay by the hour, the incentive is transactional: deliver content, clock out, invoice. The tutor has no stake in whether you pass or fail next term. Their obligation ends when the hour hand moves.

When you commit monthly, we commit back. We track your progress across tests, prelims, and finals. We adjust when you're struggling. We push harder when you're coasting. We become accountable for your outcome—not just our time.

The Journey

What Monthly Commitment Actually Looks Like

Week 1: Diagnostic & Foundation

We don't assume where you are. We assess. We identify gaps from previous grades that are sabotaging your current work. We build a personalised roadmap—not for one session, but for your entire matric year.

Week 2-3: Structured Intervention

Consistent sessions covering current curriculum while patching foundational holes. No rushing. No skipping. We teach for understanding, not for the clock.

Week 4: Assessment & Adjustment

Monthly review of progress. What's working? What isn't? We adapt. We bring in extra resources. We coordinate with parents. We don't move on until you own the material.

Across the Term: Exam & Test Revision

Our Cross Night and Cross Day studies aren't extras—they're built into the monthly commitment. When tests approach, we don't charge more. We show up more.

Across the Year: Varsity Readiness

APS score tracking. Bursary application guidance. University visit coordination. Career exhibition access. This is what monthly commitment unlocks—a bridge to your future, not just a band-aid for today.
